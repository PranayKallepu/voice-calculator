Help Center

Use these voice commands for operations:

  • plus - Addition (+) e.g., "2 plus 3"
  • minus - Subtraction (-) e.g., "10 minus 5"
  • times - Multiplication (×) e.g., "2 into 3"
  • divided by - Division (/) e.g., "10 divided by 5"
  • power - Exponentiation (e.g., "2 power 3")
  • square root - Square root (e.g., "square root of 16")
  • mod - Modulus (e.g., "10 mod 3")
  • factorial - Factorial (e.g., "factorial 5")

Examples:

  • "What is 2 plus 3 minus 4 into 5?"
